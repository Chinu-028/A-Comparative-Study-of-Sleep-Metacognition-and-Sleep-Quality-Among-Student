Title of Study: A Comparative Study of Sleep Metacognition and Sleep Quality Among Undergraduate and Postgraduate Students: The Mediating Role of Emotion Regulation

Researcher: Dissertation Student, Department of Psychology, Ravenshaw University

Invitation: You are invited to participate in a dissertation study on sleep metacognition, sleep quality, and emotion regulation among undergraduate and postgraduate students.

Procedure: Participation involves a 10-15 minute questionnaire about your sleep patterns, thoughts about sleep, and ability to regulate emotions.

Risks and Benefits: No physical or psychological risks are anticipated.

Voluntary Participation: Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time.

Anonymity: All responses are completely anonymous. A random participant ID will be generated.

Eligibility: Ravenshaw University students aged 18 to 25 years.
