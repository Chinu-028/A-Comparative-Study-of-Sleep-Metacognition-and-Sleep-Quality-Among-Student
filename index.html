Title of Study: Attitudes Towards Psychological Counselling Among Ravenshaw University Students

Researcher: Dissertation Student, Department of Psychology, Ravenshaw University

Invitation: You are invited to participate in a dissertation study on attitudes towards psychological counselling among Ravenshaw University students.

Procedure: Participation involves a brief 5-10 minute questionnaire about your views on mental health and counselling services.

Risks and Benefits: No physical or psychological risks are anticipated. While there are no direct benefits to you, your participation will contribute to understanding mental health attitudes among university students.

Voluntary Participation: Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without any penalty or consequence.

Anonymity: All responses are completely anonymous—no identifying information (name, student ID, etc.) will be collected. Data will be used solely for academic purposes and reported only in aggregate.

Eligibility: To participate, you must be a Ravenshaw University student aged 18 to 25 years.

Contact: If you have questions about this study, please contact the researcher through the Department of Psychology, Ravenshaw University.
